Runiq - Frostfare (In progress)

April 2024 - Current

FrostFare is a desktop application that automates the email warming process to improve deliverability. By ensuring emails are seen as legitimate, it helps prevent them from being marked as spam, thus enhancing the effectiveness of email marketing campaigns through better open rates and engagement.

My contribution towards Frostfare included:

  • Redesigned Key Features: Enhanced user experience by updating and refining several features, focusing on usability and interface efficiency.
  • Front-End Development: Translated Figma designs into functional interfaces, building a cohesive and user-friendly front-end.
  • Technology Implementation: Utilised React and Material-UI (MUI) extensively to develop responsive and visually appealing components.
